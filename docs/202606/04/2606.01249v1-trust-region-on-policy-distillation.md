## 摘要
策略内蒸馏（OPD）是大型语言模型（LLM）高效后训练的一项基础技术，在智能体学习、多任务增强和模型压缩等领域有广泛应用。然而，当教师模型与学生模型分布差异较大时，OPD训练会变得不稳定，因为对学生生成的词元进行的教师监督可能产生不可靠的策略梯度，甚至导致优化失败。本工作通过信用分配策略解决可靠的策略内词元级监督问题，并提出了信赖域策略内蒸馏（TrOPD）。它具有以下特点：1) 信赖域策略内学习：TrOPD仅在教师模型提供可靠监督的区域进行OPD，减轻了分布不匹配下K1反向KL估计量的优化难度。2) 离群值估计：对于离群区域，我们探索梯度裁剪、掩码及前向KL估计，以减少不可靠监督的不利影响。3) 离线策略引导：学生模型从教师模型的前缀继续生成，并使用前向KL模仿离线策略引导，鼓励策略内探索朝向可靠区域。实验表明，TrOPD在数学推理、代码生成和通用领域基准测试中，始终优于最先进的OPD基线，包括OPD、EOPD和REOPOLD。

## Abstract
On-Policy Distillation (OPD) is a fundamental technique for efficient post-training of large language models (LLMs), with broad applications in agent learning, multi-task enhancement, and model compression. However, OPD training becomes unstable when the teacher and student distributions differ substantially, as teacher supervision on student-generated tokens may yield unreliable policy gradients and even cause optimization failure. This work addresses reliable on-policy token-level supervision through credit assignment strategies, and proposes Trust Region On-Policy Distillation, TrOPD. It features the following characteristics: 1) Trust-Region On-Policy Learning: TrOPD performs OPD only in regions where the teacher provides reliable supervision, mitigating the optimization difficulty of the K1 reverse-KL estimator under distribution mismatch. 2) Outlier Estimation: For outlier regions, we explore gradient clipping, masking, and forward-KL estimation to reduce the adverse effects of unreliable supervision. 3) Off-Policy Guidance: The student continues generation from teacher prefixes and uses forward KL to imitate off-policy guidance, encouraging on-policy exploration toward reliable regions. Experiments show that TrOPD consistently outperforms SoTA OPD baselines, including OPD, EOPD, and REOPOLD, across mathematical reasoning, code generation, and general-domain benchmarks.

### 2. 方法论

- **核心思想**：将学生生成的token按监督可靠性划分为**信赖域（Trust Region）** 和**离群区域（Outlier）**，对不同区域采用不同的蒸馏目标，并辅以**离线策略引导**增强探索。
- **技术细节与公式**：
  - **信赖域划分**：基于师生模型对同一token的解码一致性概率，定义token属于信任区域的二值指示器 \( M_x \sim \text{Bernoulli}(P_{\text{trust}}(x)) \)，其中 \( P_{\text{trust}}(x) = \min\left(\frac{\pi_T(x)}{\pi_S(x)}, 1\right) \)。仿照推测解码中的接受概率，确保只在教师“同意”的区域内进行反向KL优化。
  - **token级优化目标**：
    - 在信任区域使用**K1估计量的反向KL** \( -M_x \log \frac{\pi_S}{\pi_T} \)。
    - 在离群区域使用**Top-k前向KL** \( -M_x \sum_{v \in V_T^k} \pi_T(v) \log \frac{\pi_T(v)}{\pi_S(v)} \)，从教师视角进行监督，避免极端梯度。
  - **离线策略引导**：生成轨迹由教师前缀（off-policy）和学生续写（on-policy）构成，对教师前缀部分施加**K1估计的前向KL** \( -\beta \log \frac{\pi_S}{\pi_T} \)，鼓励学生生成与教师支持的可靠区域对齐。引导系数\(\beta\)随训练进程余弦退火至0。
  - **统一损失函数**：
    \[
    J^{\text{TrOPD}}_x = -I[x \sim \pi_S] M_x \sum_{v \in V_T^k} \pi_{T,v} \log\frac{\pi_{T,v}}{\pi_{S,v}} - I[x \sim \pi_T] M_x \log\frac{\pi_S}{\pi_T} - \beta \, I[x \sim \pi_T] \log\frac{\pi_T}{\pi_S}
